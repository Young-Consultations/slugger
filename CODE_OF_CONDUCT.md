# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

Young-Consultations contributors pledge to provide a respectful, harassment-free collaboration space for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, direct, and constructive.
- Keep feedback focused on the work and its impact.
- Respect security, privacy, and confidentiality boundaries.
- Avoid public disclosure of vulnerabilities, secrets, or private customer information.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, abuse, threats, discriminatory language, doxxing, and deliberate exposure of sensitive information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or restrict participation when conduct harms the project. Report conduct concerns through a private maintainer-approved channel.
