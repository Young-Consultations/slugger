# Leadership Readout Outline

| Time | Segment | Decision Focus |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0-5 min | Purpose and scope | Confirm what was and was not assessed. |
| 5-10 min | Executive summary | Align on the main delivery risk and opportunity. |
| 10-20 min | Findings and impact | Decide which risks require leadership action. |
| 20-30 min | Recommendations | Select priority actions and owners. |
| 30-40 min | 30/60/90 roadmap | Approve sequencing, dependencies, and metrics. |
| 40-45 min | Next steps | Confirm implementation proposal or closeout path. |
