# Discovery Call Agenda

| Time | Topic | Outcome |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0-3 min | Introductions and roles | Confirm sponsor, decision maker, and facilitator. |
| 3-8 min | Business problem | State the problem, urgency, and customer impact. |
| 8-13 min | Delivery context | Identify teams, backlogs, releases, and constraints. |
| 13-18 min | Evidence availability | Confirm systems, reports, and documents available for review. |
| 18-23 min | Success criteria | Define measurable engagement outcomes and readout audience. |
| 23-28 min | Scope and next steps | Confirm scope assumptions, risks, and readiness actions. |
| 28-30 min | Decision | Decide whether to proceed, refine, or pause. |
