# Stakeholder Interview Guide

Use open-ended questions, ask for examples, and request evidence references when possible.

## Executive Leadership
* What customer or business outcome is most at risk?
* Which delivery decisions need better visibility?

## Product or Program Leadership
* How are priorities selected and changed?
* Which commitments are hardest to forecast?

## Engineering Leadership
* Where does delivery flow slow down?
* Which technical risks threaten customer outcomes?

## Developers
* What makes work ready or not ready?
* Which review, build, or dependency issues create rework?

## Test and Quality Staff
* Where are defects found and escaped?
* Which quality gates are reliable or bypassed?

## Scrum Masters or Delivery Leads
* How are sprint goals, capacity, carryover, blockers, and retrospectives managed?

## Customers or Business Representatives
* Do delivered increments match expected value and timing?
* Where is communication unclear?

## Security and Operations
* What release, incident, data, credential, and AI governance concerns affect delivery?
