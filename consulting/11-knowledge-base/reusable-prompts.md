# Reusable Prompts

Each prompt requires evidence-based output, clear assumptions, no invented facts, human review, and protection of confidential information. Remove client identifiers unless approved.

## Organize Interview Notes
Organize the provided interview notes into themes. Use only supplied evidence, list assumptions separately, do not invent facts, protect confidential information, and mark the output for human review.

## Categorize Findings
Categorize these evidence-supported observations by assessment area and risk level. Use only provided evidence, state assumptions, avoid invented facts, protect confidential information, and require human review before sharing.

## Draft Risk Statements
Draft risk statements in the format "If..., then..., resulting in..." using only the supplied evidence. Include assumptions, avoid invented facts, protect confidential information, and require human review.

## Translate Technical Issues into Customer Language
Translate these technical issues into customer and business impact language. Preserve evidence references, state assumptions, avoid invented facts, protect confidential information, and require human review.

## Draft Recommendations
Draft recommendations with owner, metric, target date, expected benefit, and definition of done. Use only evidence provided, state assumptions, avoid invented facts, protect confidential information, and require human review.

## Create Definitions of Done
Create measurable definitions of done for these roadmap actions. Tie each item to evidence and success metrics, state assumptions, avoid invented facts, protect confidential information, and require human review.

## Produce a Roadmap
Sequence recommendations into 0-30, 31-60, and 61-90 day actions. Use evidence-based priorities, state assumptions, avoid invented facts, protect confidential information, and require human review.

## Review Report Consistency
Review the report for consistency among evidence, observations, findings, recommendations, roadmap actions, and definitions of done. State assumptions, do not invent facts, protect confidential information, and require human review.
