# Consulting Operating System

## Purpose

This directory contains reusable assets for assessing and improving software delivery organizations. It supports consulting engagements that connect customer value, delivery execution, SDLC maturity, technical risk, and AI-SDLC readiness into evidence-based recommendations and measurable implementation roadmaps.

## Primary Consulting Offer

The initial offer is the **Software Delivery and AI-SDLC Alignment Assessment**. It evaluates how effectively a delivery organization turns customer and business needs into reliable software outcomes.

The assessment evaluates:

1. Customer and business alignment
2. Backlog quality and readiness
3. Prioritization effectiveness
4. Sprint planning and execution
5. Team velocity and delivery flow
6. SDLC maturity
7. Technical debt and defect management
8. Technical-to-customer communication
9. Delivery metrics and governance
10. AI-SDLC readiness and traceability

## Intended Users

* Independent software consultants
* Engineering leaders
* Delivery leads
* Program managers
* Scrum masters
* Product leaders
* AI-SDLC implementation teams

## Standard Engagement Workflow

Lead → Qualification → Discovery → Engagement Scope → Evidence Collection → Interviews → Assessment → Findings → Recommendations → 30/60/90 Roadmap → Leadership Readout → Implementation Proposal → Closeout

## Operating Principles

* Customer value drives prioritization.
* Evidence must support findings.
* Observations, conclusions, and recommendations must remain distinct.
* Every finding must include customer or business impact.
* Every recommendation must include an owner and measurable definition of done.
* AI-generated content must be human-reviewed.
* Client confidentiality and intellectual property must be protected.
* Slugger may assist the workflow but does not replace accountable human judgment.
