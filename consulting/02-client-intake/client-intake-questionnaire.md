# Client Intake Questionnaire

## Organization and Product
* What product, platform, or service is in scope?
* Who are the primary customers or users?
* What business outcomes are most important this quarter?

## Team Composition
* What roles are on the delivery team?
* Which teams, vendors, or shared services affect delivery?

## Delivery Context
* What customer commitments, deadlines, or contractual obligations are at risk?
* What delivery problems are most visible to leadership or customers?
* How would you describe backlog condition, sprint performance, technical debt, testing, release practices, and defect trends?

## Metrics and AI Usage
* Which delivery metrics are reviewed today?
* How are AI tools used for requirements, design, coding, testing, review, or documentation?
* What security, compliance, data handling, or IP rules apply?

## Commercial Fit
* What desired business outcome would make this engagement successful?
* Who owns budget and decision authority?
* What timeline, kickoff date, and readout date are preferred?
